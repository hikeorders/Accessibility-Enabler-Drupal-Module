INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS

1. Upload this module into your /modules directory on your Drupal site.

2. Enable the module using the "Modules" admin page on your site

3. Visit the module's configuration page. (http://www.example.com/admin/config/system/a11yenabler)

4. Make sure that "Enable Accessibility Enabler" is checked.

5. Enter your OrgID into the OrgID field. Your OrgID is available during on-boarding process in your Accessibility Enabler account.

6. Save your settings.
